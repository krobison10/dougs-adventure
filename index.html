Controls

Use keys W, A, S, D to move

Select hotbar slots with the number keys

Press Ctrl+F to toggle the FPS counter


Guide

Defeat enemies to gain items and boosts that increase Doug's strength.

There exists a number of bosses that are advisable to fight in a certain order:

First you should defeat the bear, it roams the forest, hungry. Tales tell that if
one defeats it they may be able to possess a tool which allows them to attack
from afar.

The next boss breathes fire and flies high in the skies to the northeast.
Defeating it will allow one to obtain a weapon which harnesses their inner mana.

The final boss comes from the depths of hell and patrols the southwest. It must
be defeated for Doug to escape this world and return home. Does he have what it takes?

Enemies in the world will drop arrows, health potions, life crystals to
increase maximum life, and mana crystals to increase maximum mana.
Tip: a certain winged enemy that only emerges at night is known to yield
mana crystals at a high rate.

Beware the wolves at night, the darkness emboldens then to move very swiftly

On some nights, the blood moon will rise. Beware, as the number of enemies will be
greatly increased.
